# Smiles Forever Dental Clinic

## About Us
Smiles Forever Dental Clinic is a modern dental facility committed to providing high-quality, comprehensive dental care in a comfortable and welcoming environment. Our team of experienced dentists and staff are dedicated to helping you achieve and maintain optimal oral health with a blend of traditional and advanced dental techniques.

## Facility Summary
Established in 2005, Smiles Forever Dental Clinic has grown to become one of the leading dental care providers in the Delhi NCR region. Our state-of-the-art facilities include:

- 5 fully-equipped treatment rooms
- Advanced 3D imaging and digital X-ray technology
- In-house dental laboratory for custom prosthetics
- Sterilization center adhering to international standards
- Comfortable waiting area with entertainment for children
- Wheelchair accessibility and special needs accommodations
- Parking facility for patients

We pride ourselves on maintaining a hygienic, modern, and calming environment to ensure the best possible experience for our patients.

Smiles Forever Dental Clinic observes the following public holidays, during which the clinic will be closed:

1. Republic Day (January 26)
2. Holi (Date varies, usually in March)
3. Good Friday (Date varies, usually in April)
4. Independence Day (August 15)
5. Gandhi Jayanti (October 2)
6. Dussehra (Date varies, usually in October)
7. Diwali (Date varies, usually in October/November)
8. Christmas (December 25)

Please note:
- On other regional holidays, the clinic may operate with reduced hours. Please check our website or call our office for current hours.
- During festival seasons, our operating hours may be adjusted. We recommend confirming your appointment time in advance.

Emergency Services during Holidays:
For dental emergencies during public holidays, please call our emergency hotline at +91 98765 43210. Our on-call dentist will assess your situation and provide guidance or arrange for emergency care if necessary.

## Our Team

### Dr. Arun Patel, MDS - General Dentist and Clinic Director
Dr. Patel completed his MDS in Conservative Dentistry and Endodontics from Maulana Azad Institute of Dental Sciences, New Delhi. With over 15 years of experience, he specializes in microscope-assisted RCT and aesthetic dentistry. Dr. Patel has been awarded the "Best Dental Surgeon" by the Indian Dental Association, Delhi Branch in 2020.

### Dr. Priya Sharma, MDS - Orthodontist
Dr. Sharma earned her MDS in Orthodontics from King George's Medical University, Lucknow. She has 10 years of experience in handling complex orthodontic cases and is certified in Invisalign and lingual braces. Dr. Sharma regularly conducts workshops on modern orthodontic techniques.

### Dr. Rajesh Kumar, MDS - Periodontist
With an MDS in Periodontics from Manipal College of Dental Sciences, Dr. Kumar brings 12 years of expertise in gum health and dental implants. He is known for his minimally invasive techniques in periodontal surgeries and has published several papers in international dental journals.

### Dr. Sunita Gupta, MDS - Endodontist
Dr. Gupta completed her MDS in Conservative Dentistry and Endodontics from Nair Hospital Dental College, Mumbai. With 8 years of experience, she specializes in microscope-assisted root canal treatments and dental trauma management. Dr. Gupta is a certified trainer for rotary endodontics systems.

### Ms. Anjali Singh - Dental Hygienist
Anjali has a Bachelor's degree in Dental Hygiene from Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi. With 6 years of experience, she is expert in providing thorough yet gentle cleanings and educating patients on optimal oral hygiene practices.

### Mr. Vikram Malhotra - Dental Assistant
Vikram is a certified dental assistant with 5 years of experience. He completed his training from Delhi Paramedical and Management Institute and is skilled in four-handed dentistry, radiography, and patient care.
